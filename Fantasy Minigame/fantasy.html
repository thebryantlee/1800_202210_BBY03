Fantasy Minigame - How to Play

Find your athlete

Scroll down the list of athletes and teams, and their respective events on the page to view the different predictions you can choose from!

Each athlete and team has a different prediction based on their event. Some may be finishing in a certain position or achieving a specific goal in their event.
