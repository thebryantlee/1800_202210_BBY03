Curling

Curling is a sport in which players slide stones on a sheet of ice toward a target area which is segmented into four concentric circles. It is related to bowls, boules, and shuffleboard.

Canada's Gushue wins fifth in a row at men's world curling championship

rad Gushue's curling team started to find its groove Monday in a 10-4 win over Italy to stay unbeaten at the world men's curling championship. Canada was alone atop the standings at 5-0 after its lone game Monday. Gushue, Mark Nichols, Brett Gallant and Geoff Walker out of St. John's, N.L., take on Germany on Tuesday.

Chelsea Carey's curling team added to list of rinks disbanding at season's end

Chelsea Carey's curling team has joined a run of others in deciding to disband at the conclusion of this season. Carey, a two-time Canadian women's champion, skipped a five-player team out of Regina including Jennifer Armstrong, Jolene Campbell, Rachel Erickson and Stephanie Schmidt.

Team Gunnlaugson disbanding at end of season

Gunnlaugson, 37, has skipped the foursome of third Adam Casey, second Matt Wozniak and lead Connor Njegovan for the past two seasons, appearing in the Tim Hortons Brier each year.
